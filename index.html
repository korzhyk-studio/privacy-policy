Privacy Policy — Korzhyk Studio

Effective Date: 06.08.2025

1. General Provisions

We respect the privacy of all users of our applications, especially children. Our applications are intended for children aged 3 years and older and strictly comply with applicable international and national data protection laws, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA, USA);
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, EU);
  • Law of Ukraine "On the Protection of Personal Data";
  • Other applicable rules governing the processing of personal data.

We strive to ensure safe, transparent and minimized data processing.

2. Data Collection

Personal data that we DO NOT collect:

  • Username, address, email;
  • Precise geolocation data;
  • Unique identifiers that allow you to identify the child.

Analytics and anonymized data: The application may use third-party services (e.g. Google Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase) to collect anonymized information for purposes such as:

  • Improving application performance;
  • Diagnosing errors and crashes;
  • Displaying ads that meet children's standards.

Such data may include:

  • Device type, model, operating system;
  • Interface language and country;
  • Length of game sessions;
  • Generalized technical logs (e.g. error codes).

We do not use this data to identify individuals and do not profile users.

3. In-app purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to payment details (e.g. card number), but we may have access to:

  • Google account name;
  • Email address;
  • Purchase information (transaction ID, amount, date).

This data is used exclusively to confirm the transaction and provide paid content.

More information: Google Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

Google AdMob is used to display ads. When children use our products, we:

  • Disable personalized advertising;
  • Enable the “child-directed” mode;
  • Use only certified advertising networks that comply with COPPA requirements.

We do not allow the collection of users' personal data for displaying ads.

More information: AdMob Policy

5. Processing of children's personal information

We do not collect personal information directly from children. If such information is provided as part of a request from a parent or guardian, we use it only to:

  • Provide the requested support;
  • Verify the request;
  • Protect the rights and safety of the child.

Such information may only be transferred in the following cases:

  • As required by law or court;
  • To ensure user safety;
  • To protect against fraud;
  • At the request of law enforcement agencies within the framework of applicable law.

6. Age determination

When the application is first launched, the user is prompted to specify their age:

  • If less than 13 years — the child mode is automatically enabled, blocking personalized advertising and other functions that require data collection;
  • If 13 years and older — we assume that the entered age is correct.

If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe that the child has entered an incorrect age, please contact us and we will delete the collected data if necessary.

7. Rights of data subjects

You or your legal representative have the right to exercise the following rights in accordance with applicable law:

  • Right to access data;
  • Right to rectification or updating;
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten");
  • Right to restriction of processing;
  • Right to object to processing;
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time;
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

We will review and implement your request within 30 days of identity verification, unless:

  • Your identity cannot be verified;
  • The request jeopardizes the rights of other users;
  • There are legitimate grounds for storing the data;
  • The request is excessive or unfounded.

8. Registration and accounts

The Application does not provide for registration, account creation or authorization. Accordingly, we do not process:

  • Logins;
  • Passwords;
  • Email addresses for registration purposes.

9. Data Security

We use technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • SDK-level encryption;
  • Use of official and trusted services (Google, Firebase, AdMob);
  • Restricting access to data;
  • Regularly updating the application to eliminate vulnerabilities.

10. Parental Control

Parents are encouraged to participate in the first launch of the application and selecting the child's age. We rely on the entered data. If errors or violations are detected, please contact us — we will delete the relevant information, if any, was collected.

11. Limitation of Liability

We make reasonable efforts to comply with legal requirements. However, we are not responsible for:

  • Incorrect age specified by the user;
  • Actions of third-party services (e.g. Google) that are not under our control.

  • Via an application update;
  • Via an update description in the Google Play store.

The date of the last update is always indicated at the top of the document.
